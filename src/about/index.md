# About

A little bit about myself...

I was born in Albania, raised in Greece and eventually migrated to the United States, where I currently live.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from Wentworth Institute of Technology and a Master of Science in Sofware Engineering from Kennesaw State University.

I have worked in the semiconductor industry almost my entire career, specifically in the memory and storage areas, doing mostly automation work for firmware and firmware validation teams. The most rewarding part of my job is enabling workflows which allow product development to proceed smoothly; in other words, adding value to the broader group allows me to extract satisfaction from my day-to-day tasks.

Outside of work I like spending time with my family, playing the guitar, going on walks, reading books or comics, playing video games. Media wise I am probably consuming more podcasts than anything else these days.
